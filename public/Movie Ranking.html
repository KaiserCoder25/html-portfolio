The Best Animes According To Vince Russel Antonio

My Top 3 Animes

1. Naruto

This is an anime I started to love as a child. Though the series was incomplete from the television channel where it was introduced to me,I did find my way to get to watch the rest of the story when I grew up

2. Freirin

One of the best modern animes. It is unlike the others that simply follows the same format of a protagonist working there way up to be strong enough to beat the adversaries. The story already started in an aftermat of a war where the leader of the enemy is already dead. It's a cool showcase of "what if" you live a thousand years in an eye of an elf who basically grows old very slowly. How are sad things can heal thru time and how to live in the moment

Bofuri

An anime in a virtual reality game setting. A simple yet adventurous little girl that want to play but doesn't want to get hurt. So what she did was put her stats all on defense.
